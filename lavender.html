LAVENDER

The scent of Lavender has been a favorite for thousands of years, and it isn't a surprise that it is still a popular stress reliever to this day.It is known as the "universal oil" and boasts many benefits when inhaled, such as improvement in short-term memory, anxiety, relief, and relaxation.

CARE

Lavender prefers a lot of sunlight, preferably placed in a bright south facing window.

Lavender does not need to be watered often, however you must pay close attention to overwatering. Only water when the soil is completely dry, overwatering will cause root rot!
