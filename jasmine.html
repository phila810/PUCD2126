JASMINE

Jasmine is widely renowned for its uplifting aroma that can stimulate your mind and body. If you are feeling down, this wonderful scent can help you to rise back up again with its confidence boosting abilities. The fragrance of Jasmine has been described as powerful, sweet, and floral so it is likely to leave your home smelling amazing!

CARE

Jasmine is pretty lenient when it comes to sunlight conditions. It does not prefer direct sunlight, and can grow in a shadier area of your home, or a natural lit area.

Jasmine is a bit tricky when it comes to watering. It perfers to be underwatered than overwatered. Its roots are extremely sensative, and prone to root rot.
