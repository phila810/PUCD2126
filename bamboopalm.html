BAMBOO PALM

Also known as the Reed Palm, this plant is a fantastic air purifier. Ditch your chemical-laden air fresheners in favor of a few of these pretty palms and say goodbye to airborne smells and toxins!

CARE

The Bamboo Palm does not require much sunlight, and should not be placed in direct sunlight. A naturally lit room will do just fine.

Water your palm when the first inch of soil is dry. Avoid overwatering! Too much water will cause a lot of moisture, leading to root rot.
