ALOE VERA

Aloe Vera releases oxygen and absorbs carbon dioxide making breathing a whole lot smoother and healthier inside of your home. It also clears formaldehyde and benzene which are byproducts of common chemical household cleaners.

CARE

These plants should be placed near a sunny window where it will receive direct sunlight at some point during the day. Avoic placing in the shade.

Aloe Vera is a succulent, which means it is extremely low maintenence when it comes to watering. Water once a week to every other week. Better to underwater these plants than to overwater!
