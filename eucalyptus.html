EUCALYPTUS

Leaves of the Eucalyptus plant are filled with tannis which raises healthy fluids in the body's air passages. Just breathing in the scent of these plants can help to lower congestion problems and ward off colds!

CARE

Eucalyptus require some sort of sunlight. Place in a room that is naturally lit and receives sun for a large portion of the day.

Water your plant whenever you notice that the top layer of soil is dry. Add water until it seeps fully to the bottom of the pot.
