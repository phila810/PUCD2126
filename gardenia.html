GARDENIA

With glossy evergreen leaves and beautifully scented blossoms, Gardenias are a popular plant choice for bedrooms. Studies indicate that keeping one in your room may help you achieve a better quality of sleep, with claims that it may be as effective as Valium in relieving anxiety and promoting sleep.

CARE

Gardenias need a lot of sunlight, specifically direct sunlight for at least half of the day. However, Gardenias love humidity. This is a bit difficult to balance, but the easiest way to maintain humidity is to spray the plant once daily.

Due to the Gardenia's tricky humidity needs, moisture is a huge part in keeping your plant healthy. Water whenever the top layer of soil feels dry.
